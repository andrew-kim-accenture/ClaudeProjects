The Customers concept from Quote 1.0 has been obsolete in Quote 2.0 and the same results can be achieved using Business Partners. Since Customers and Business Partners are conceptually different, migration of any kind is not possible. Instead, you need to implement Business Partners from scratch in Quote 2.0.
