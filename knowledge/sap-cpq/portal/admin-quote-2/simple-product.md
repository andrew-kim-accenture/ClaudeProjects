Simple Product

A Simple Product doesn't require any attributes/options selected to be added to a quote. Because of this, the Configuration Layout and Rules tabs stay inactive when you choose the Simple Product display type.

You can add attributes to a simple product for additional properties like color, material, and size. These attributes help with product comparison or detail pages. For more information, see Product Details Templates.

Simple products and their attributes are usually imported through the Product Import. For more details, see Bulk Product Import/Export.

From the Catalog, you can select and add simple products to a quote. By default, they don't show a Configure button. However, you can display an inactive Configure button by adjusting the Show customize button for simple products setting in Setup  General  Application Parameters  Product Catalog tab.

When added to a quote, a simple product appears as a single main item. Only the Copy and Delete actions (Item Actions Workflow) are available in the quote.
