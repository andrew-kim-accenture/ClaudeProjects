Bulk Delete Business Partners

You can Bulk delete business partners from the Business Partner page of the Setup.

Delete Multiple Business Partners.

Go to Setup  Business Partners.

Select multiple Business Partners.

Once selected, click Delete.

A confirmation window appears, click Yes to delete the Business Partners.

Delete Multiple Business Partners from multiple pages

Go to Setup  Business Partners.

Choose multiple Business Partners, for example, filter table by name and select the checkbox on the header to select all the displayed Business Partners and click Delete to delete all the selected business partners at once.

A confirmation window appears, click Yes to delete the Business Partners.

The deletion of the business partners is logged in the Audit trail.
