Block Business Partners from Being Deleted

You can prevent business partners from being edited or deleted.

To prevent customers from being edited or deleted:

Go to Setup  Security  Data Deletion.

A new page displays.

In Blocked Data  Blocked Business Partners, enter either the first name, last name, or email address of the business partner you want to block from deletion.

The field offers autocomplete suggestions as you start typing a search query. Business partners matching the input are displayed as suggestions. Alternatively, you can select a business partner from the dropdown list.

Click Save.

A confirmation message displays.
