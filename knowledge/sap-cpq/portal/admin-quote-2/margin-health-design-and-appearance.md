Margin Health Design and Appearance

On this page, you can define the icon that displays on quotes as the margin health indicator, as well as the color of the icon, on both quote engines.

The default colors and icons are applied if you don’t select other colors and upload other icons. If you’ve already uploaded a new icon, you can use the default one again by clicking Restore Default. Have in mind when uploading your own images that they should fit the size of the Margin Health column on the quote. There are no restrictions on the supported format of uploaded images.

You can control the visibility of margin icons when the list price or list cost is zero using the toggle buttons and dropdowns labeled For items where list price is zero, display margin picture as and For items where cost is zero, display margin picture as.
