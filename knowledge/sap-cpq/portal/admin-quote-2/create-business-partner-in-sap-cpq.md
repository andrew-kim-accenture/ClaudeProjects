Create Business Partner in SAP CPQ

Business partners are created and managed in a dedicated section in the Setup by administrators with enabled access rights for this feature.

Procedure
In Setup, go to Business Partners  Business Partners.
Click Add New Business Partner.

A page with General and Permissions tabs displays.

Fill out the fields in the General tab as explained in Business Partners General Fields table.
(Optional) If business partner custom fields have been created, fill them out in the Custom Fields tab.
Save the changes.

The business partner is created.

Note

You can manage the permissions for business partners in the Business Partner Permissions section. Before any business partner permission rules are defined, no business partners are accessible to any users.
