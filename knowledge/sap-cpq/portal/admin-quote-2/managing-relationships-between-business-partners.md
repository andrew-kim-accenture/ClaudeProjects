Managing Relationships Between Business Partners

Navigate to Setup, choose Business Partners   Business Partner.

Locate the business partner you want to establish a Business Partner Relationship with, and click Edit.

On the Relationship tab, click Add New.

On the Add New Business Partner Relationship screen, enter values in the Relationship Category and the Business Partner field. Enable the Default toggle if you wish this relationship to be the default one for the selected business partner.Note that this information is visible on the quote. For the default tag to be displayed, the business partners must first be filtered by the relationship on business partner search model. In the list of all business partners, the default business partner will have the text (Default) displayed next to their name, making it easy to identify the primary relationship.

Click Add.

Once the new relationship is saved, a new row is displayed in Business Partner Relationship section.

You can delete the relationship. If you delete a relationship with a business partner, the same relationship will also be deleted from the connected business partner. If you delete a business partner, all of their relationships will also be deleted. Every deletion and creation is logged in the Audit trail.
