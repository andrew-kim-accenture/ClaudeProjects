General Settings

The following topics explain the general settings in SAP CPQ.



Time Zones

If your company has departments in different countries/regions, in different time zones, or simply your users are scattered around the world, SAP CPQ can be set up SAP CPQ to display information in their own time zone.
Custom Tabs

Custom tabs are components that help organize information on some of the SAP CPQ pages. You can customize tabs to display the information best suited to your business needs.
