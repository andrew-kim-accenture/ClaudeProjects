Search, Edit and Delete Business Partner

If you add only a partner function without a business partner in the Involved parties tab, you can afterwards search for and add a business partner directly from the grid.

Additionally, you can remove a business partner by clicking  next to it in the grid. If the partner is created by the administrator in the Setup, this will only remove the partner from the quote, it won’t delete it. If the business partner is created locally, only for that partner function, removing it from the grid removes it altogether.

Once a business partner is added to a quote, the relation between the business partner on the quote and that business partner in the Setup no longer exists. In other words, if you edit a business partner in the Involved parties tab, the changes are not propagated in the Setup. Likewise, if you edit a business partner in the Setup, the changes are not propagated to existing quotes.
