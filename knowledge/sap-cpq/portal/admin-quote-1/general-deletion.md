General Deletion

It's possible to define time periods after which user, quote, customer, and business partner data is deleted.

On the General Deletion tab, you define a time period for:

Automatic deletion of quotes

Automatic deletion of users

Automatic deletion of customers

When Users, Quotes, or Customers are deleted using the Data Deletion functionality from CPQ Setup, the Audit Trail logs will display GDPR - delete... in the Action column, while in the User column System User will be displayed as the user that performed the that action.



Automatic Deletion of Customers

Automatic customer deletion applies to both global and local customers.
Automatic Deletion of Quotes

When deleting quotes, you have three options at your disposal.
Automatic Deletion of Users

When deleting users, you can either delete inactive users or users who don’t log into the system during the specified deletion period.
