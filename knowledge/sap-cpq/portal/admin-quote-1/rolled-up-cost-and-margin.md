Rolled-Up Cost and Margin

This feature gives users ability to use rolled-up cost and margin on the item level.

Rolled-up cost is displayed on the main item level and represents sum of main item and line items costs. The same applies for rolled-up margin.

Editing rolled-up net price

When a user edits rolled-up net price discount is recalculated for all (main and line) items

Example:

Product total price is 1200 and product cost is 720. Rolled-up margin is 40%. User edit rolled-up net price and enters 1000. Discount is calculated for all line items to 16.67%. Rolled-up GM is calculated to 28%.

Editing rolled-up gross margin

When a user edits rolled-up gross margin discount is recalculated for all (main and line) items.

Example:

Product total price is 1200 and product cost is 720. Rolled-up margin is 40%. User edit rolled-up gross margin and enters 25%. Discount is calculated for all line items to 20%. Rolled-up price is calculated to 960.
