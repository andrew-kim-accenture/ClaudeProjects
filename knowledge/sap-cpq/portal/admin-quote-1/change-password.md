Change Password

After logging in, you can easily change your password in User Page or in user settings in Setup. We advise changing the password regularly to ensure better security and protect the privacy of your data.

User Page

Click your name in the top-right corner.

Click User Page in the menu.

Enter your username, the current and the new password.

Click Change Password.

A confirmation message displays.

Setup

In Users, administrators can create temporary passwords for users or define password expiration periods.
