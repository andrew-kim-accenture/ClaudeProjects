Create a Simple Product

This procedure explains the minimum requirements to create a simple product in SAP CPQ.

Click Setup  Products   Products .

Click Add New Product.

Enter the product name.

The system ID is automatically populated.

Choose Simple Product.

Add the product to a category by clicking Add/Remove for Product Categories.

Open the Pricing tab and under Pricing Type, select Custom Pricing.

Define the prices and costs of the product.

Save the data.

The product is available in the catalog to be added to quotes.
