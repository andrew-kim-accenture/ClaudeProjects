Compare Quotes and Revisions (Quote 2.0)

Quote and revision comparison functionality enables you to compare two revisions of the same quote or two different quotes. You have the flexibility to choose the fields, quote tables, and the items to compare. This functionality is available for Quote 2.0.

You can turn on the Enable Comparing Quotes and Revisions parameter from the list of parameters on the General tab under Setup( Setup>General>Application Parameters>Shopping carts and Quotes). Once the parameter is enabled, the quotes and revisions comparison feature is available on the quote list, list of revisions and the quote.
Note

The Enable Comparing Quotes and Revisions is enabled by default.

When you choose the compare option, a new page is displayed. On this comparison page, you can select the quotes/revisions and choose the fields, quote tables, and the items that you want to compare. You will be able to compare quotes which are only visible to you.
Note

If no selections are made, nothing will be visible on the final comparison page.

To compare items, you must select the items from the first and then from the second quote. Once selected, click Add Pair. You can generate comparisons for up to five pairs.

To see the compared fields side-by-side, click Compare. Fields with different values will be highlighted. You can filter comparison results by selecting the toggle, which will show only the results with different values. Fields and Quote tables which are not available to you due to permissions will have the value, This field is not visible in this status.

When you compare again, all the fields and the quote tables are saved from the previous selection except items. Item details depend on the quotation and the revision selected.

Admins can turn off this functionality on the tenant level by changing the value of the parameter Enable Comparing Quotes and Revisions to false.
