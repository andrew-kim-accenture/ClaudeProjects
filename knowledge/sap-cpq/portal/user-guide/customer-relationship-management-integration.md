Customer Relationship Management Integration

The SAP CPQ – Customer Relationship Management (CRM) integration is supported for both SAP CPQ Quote 1.0 and SAP CPQ Quote 2.0 and supports quoting functionality by boosting sales efficiency, improving quote accuracy, personalizing the customer experience, and improving data driven decision making.

When you create a quote and navigate to either Generate Documents or Generate Documents for E-Sign, on the Download Document and Send Email page you can choose to send the quote to the CRM system.

To send a quote to the CRM system, follow the same process for either Generate Documents or Generate Documents for E-Sign. After selecting Generate and adding all the information on the Download Document and Send Email page, select Send to CRM from the available integrations on the page footer. If Send to CRM is the only available integration, you can use the Send to CRM button. If more than one integration is available, the first available integration is shown as a button while other integrations are available in the 3 dot menu next to the first integration button. If the quote is successfully released to sent to the CRM system, a success message will be displayed at the top of the page. If you receive the error message Document cannot be uploaded to the CRM opportunity, because the quote (quote number) has not been attached to any opportunity, contact your administrator that the CRM integration has been completed and all required mappings have been saved in SAP CPQ setup process and that the quote status is set to the relevant status for sending a quote to the CRM system.
