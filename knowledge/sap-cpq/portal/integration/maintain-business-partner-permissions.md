Maintain Business Partner Permissions

According to the Permission groups maintained in SAP CPQ, the Business Partners can be used in the quote creation.

Procedure

Login to SAP CPQ system using an Administrator user.

In the left menu bar, choose Setup  Setup.

In left navigation bar, choose Business Partners  Business Partner Permissions.

Choose Add New and maintain the following details:

Name, for example as <Integration>

Active is turned on

User Type, choose for example, <Sales>

All business partners are visible to selected groups is turned on

Choose Save.
