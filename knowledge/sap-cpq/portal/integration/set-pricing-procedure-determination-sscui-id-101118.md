Set Pricing Procedure Determination (SSCUI ID: 101118)

To assign the Pricing Procedure Determination to Sales Organisation follow the steps as below:

Test Step #

	

Test Step Name

	

Instruction

	

Expected Result

	

Pass/Fail/Comment




1

	

Log On

	

Log on to the SAP Fiori Launchpad with your user credentials as Configuration Expert - Business Process Configuration role assigned.

	

The Fiori Launchpad is displayed.

	 


2

	

Access the Manage Your Solution App

	

Open Manage Your Solution and then choose Configure Your Solution.

	

The Manage Your Solution screen is displayed.

	 


3

	

Access App Make Specifications

	

Search with following criteria:

Application Area: Sales

Sub Application Area: Price Management

Choose the group Set Pricing Procedure Determination

Assign the created Pricing procedure to respective Sales Organization, Document Pricing procedure and Customer Pricing Procedure as per requirement.

Choose Save.
Note

If the delivered Pricing procedure is already assigned to required Document pricing procedure, replace the entry as per requirement. The Document Classification used for Solution Order is S6.

	

The Pricing Procedure Determination is maintained.
