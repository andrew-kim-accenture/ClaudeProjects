Once you reviewed the prerequisites, follow the steps below to configure all three systems needed for the integration: SAP Sales Cloud, SAP CPQ, and SAP Cloud Integration.

To complete these requirements, you will need to contact different teams at SAP. For example, the SAP Cloud Integration team provides the URL and the endpoints needed for the Providers page in SAP CPQ.
