As of 2505: The Setter for NumberOfItems Will Be Removed.

Description

The setter for NumberOfItems will be removed from Scripting.Quote.IQuoteProductType. This is due to the fact that it must be a calculated property, not manually set.

Actions to Take

Clients must adjust their scripts to not use NumberOfItems setter.
