As of 2505: Obsoleting the Customer Object on Quote 2.0

Description

SAP CPQ plans to make the Customer object on Quote 2.0 obsolete starting in version 2505. SAP CPQ is offering support in migrating the Customer object to Business Partners on Quote 2.0.

Actions to Take
Contact SAP support if you need assistance with migration.
