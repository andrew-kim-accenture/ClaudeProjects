Subscription Instructions

With each new release of SAP CPQ, we send out a "What's New" document with the latest updates. Additionally, when something requires clients' action or immediate attention, we send out a corresponding announcement. To receive all these updates, subscribe to notifications.

Subscriptions are managed in Cloud System Notification Subscriptions (CSNS).

Instructions for subscribing are available on this page. Check the videos under the Cloud System Notification Subscriptions section and the FAQ.
