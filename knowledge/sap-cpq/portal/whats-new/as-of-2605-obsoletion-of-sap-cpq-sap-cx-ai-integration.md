As of 2605: Obsoletion of SAP CPQ - SAP CX AI Integration

Related Feature

SAP CX AI Integration

Who Is Affected?

Customers who have integrated their SAP CPQ tenant with the SAP CX AI service.

Description

SAP CX AI is discontinued and permanently shuts down on April 30, 2026. As a result, SAP CPQ no longer supports the SAP CPQ - SAP CX AI integration.

The SAP CX AI service had several limitations that drove this decision:

Limited functionality: it could not reliably handle complex use cases.

No extensibility: there were no options for customer customization or extensibility.

Outdated technology: the service depended on deprecated technology.

Limited compatibility: interoperability with other SAP products was incomplete.

This change does not remove AI capabilities from SAP CPQ. Instead, it allows focus on more advanced, strategically aligned Business AI solutions that will offer more sophisticated, extensible, and flexible models than SAP CX AI.

Actions to Take

No action is required.
