As of 2308: SAP CPQ Tenant URL Change Adoption End - Risk of Production Down

Description

As announced previously, the adoption period of the new SAP CPQ tenant URL ends with the 2308 release.

Caution

All customers have to adapt to the new URL format by the time 2308 goes to production or they will risk a production system downtime.

Actions to Take

Please make the mandatory changes described on the SAP Help Portal under SAP CPQ Tenant URL.

Notify the SAP CPQ support if you can foresee an issue regarding this timeline.
