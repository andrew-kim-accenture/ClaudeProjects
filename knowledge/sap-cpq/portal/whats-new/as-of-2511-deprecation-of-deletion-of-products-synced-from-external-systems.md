As of 2511: Deprecation of Deletion of Products Synced from External Systems

Related Feature

Quote 1.0 and Quote 2.0 Bulk Product Import/Export

Description

Starting with the SAP CPQ release in November 2025 (2511 release), the ability to delete products that have been synced from external systems via the Bulk Product Import functionality will be deprecated. This change is being implemented because these products are not originally created within CPQ; they are managed entirely by external systems. Deletion of these products is also forbidden in the CPQ Setup product list. This ensures that the integrity and management of these products remain under the control of the originating systems, preventing any unauthorized deletions within CPQ.

Actions to Take

No action is required from your side.
