As of 2608: Sunset of the Customers Concept in Quote 2.0

Related Feature

Migration to Quote 2.0: Customers/Customer Roles

Who Is Affected?

Customers who are using the Customers concept instead of Business Partners in either productive or sandbox environments.

We've identified several tenants that still use the Customers concept on the Quote 2.0 engine instead of Business Partners. Our team will contact all affected customers.

Description

The Customers concept from Quote 1.0 is obsolete in Quote 2.0, and you can now achieve the same or better results using Business Partners. With each new release, SAP CPQ Quote 2.0 continues to establish more integrated scenarios with SAP S/4 HANA and other SAP solutions. The implementation of Business Partners is a prerequisite for these integrations. Therefore, there's now a growing need to sunset the Customers concept on the Quote 2.0 engine and maintain a clean slate.

Actions to Take

If you are using the Customers concept in Quote 2.0, you need to implement Business Partners from scratch in Quote 2.0 before the 2608 release. Since Customers and Business Partners are conceptually different, migration of any kind isn't possible.
