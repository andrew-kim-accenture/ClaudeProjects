As of 2408: Deprecation of Property Label on Scripting.Quote.IQuoteItemCustomField

Description

The Property Label on Scripting.Quote.IQuoteItemCustomField will be removed.

Actions to Take

Adjust the scripts to ensure that the mentioned property is not being used. If the scripts are not modified, an error will occur when executing them. This change originated from the removal of the Label field from the custom page of the Quote item. It has been determined that this field serves no purpose; hence, it was eliminated from the system.
